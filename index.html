GDPR May 2018

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) will come into effect in May 2018. There is much discussion and concern about the new powers given to the Data Protection Officers around Europe.

Current Legislation and the new GDPR both put requirements on those Controlling and Processing Data. We can help you assess your current policies and procedures and identify how ready you are to move forward with a new internal process changes or a new customer opportunity.

Data Controllers and Data Processors must show compliance under the new GDPR rules and we can independently review your systems and procedures.

We're here to help you address the new requirements and to help you meet your commitments under the legislation.

Micheal O'Keeffe is Certified Data Protection Professional accredited by the Irish Computer Society. He's here to help you work with the existing legislation and prepare for the May 2018 deadline.
